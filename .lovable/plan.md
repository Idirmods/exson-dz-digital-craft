

## Exson DZ — Multi-Page Business Website

A professional multi-page website for Exson DZ, an Algerian telecom, IT, and security company. The design will replicate their deep navy blue / orange color palette and industrial-tech aesthetic.

### Color Palette & Design
- **Primary**: Deep navy blue (#0a1f44) — backgrounds, headers, hero sections
- **Accent**: Orange (#e8621c) — CTAs, highlights, logo accents
- **Light backgrounds**: White/light gray for content sections
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif with professional spacing
- **Aesthetic**: Tech network patterns, clean lines, corporate feel

### Pages

#### 1. Home Page
- **Hero slider** cycling through 4 slides: Telecom Networks, Video Surveillance, Studies & Monitoring, Access Control — each with headline, description, and two CTA buttons ("En savoir plus" / "Nous contacter")
- **Partner logos bar** (Cisco, Panasonic, D-Link, HP placeholders)
- **"Qui sommes nous"** intro section with image and brief description
- **Services overview** — 4 cards: Télécommunications, Ascenseurs, Réseaux informatiques, Systèmes de sécurité
- **Client logos carousel** (scrolling marquee)
- **Stats counter section** — Year founded (2005), Years of experience, Projects completed, Professional clients
- **Contact CTA banner**

#### 2. About Page ("Qui sommes nous")
- Company history and mission since 2005
- Values and expertise highlights
- Team/company image section

#### 3. Services Page ("Nos métiers")
- Overview of all service categories with detailed descriptions
- Sub-sections: Telecommunications, IT Networks, Security Systems (Video Surveillance, Access Control), Elevators, Studies & Monitoring

#### 4. Contact Page
- Contact form (name, email, phone, message)
- Company address: 72 bis, rue de la gare, Akbou, Bejaia
- Phone numbers and email addresses
- Embedded map placeholder
- Social media links (Facebook, LinkedIn)

### Shared Components
- **Navbar**: Exson logo (left), nav links (Home, Qui sommes nous, Nos métiers dropdown, Contact) — sticky, white background
- **Footer**: Dark navy background with logo, address, phone, email, social links
- **Responsive**: Mobile hamburger menu, fully responsive layout
- **Language**: All content in French, matching the original site
